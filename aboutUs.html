Our

It started with our love and longing with the distinctive taste of Japanese cuisine. We feel that the extraordinary taste of Japanese cuisine should be shared with the Indonesian people. Therefore, we decided to form a restaurant in October 2016 with the name Foodies.

Foodies is committed to serving high quality cuisine with great taste. The materials used are not arbitrary, come from local farmers and fishermen. Besides being able to control directly, it can also help them to develop. Because one of Foodies' vision is to grow together.

With this vision, we decided to set up an academy that would later be able to help people learn how to cook Japanese food.

And our efforts and commitments have succeeded in bringing Foodies to development and being known by the wider community. We also managed to build two more branches, namely in Jakarta and Ponorogo.

Now, we also want our restaurant to be better known and to be the best Japanese restaurant in Indonesia.

Our

Bali

Foodies was first established in October 2016 on the island of Bali. This is because Bali is a very busy tourism area, so it is hoped that Foodies will develop faster.

The Foodies restaurant in Bali is designed with a luxurious concept and is also thick with Japanese culture. Most visible from the restaurant ornaments used.

Jakarta

After successfully operating in Bali, Foodies began to open branches in the Jakarta area. Jakarta is the second place of choice because of its large population.

The design of the Foodies Jakarta restaurant is also very luxurious and features a Japanese accent. This is because Jakarta is the center of the economy as well as business transactions.

Our

Fruits and Vegetables

Foodies are very concerned about the quality of the food they make. Therefore, we use fresh and high quality fruits and vegetables. The fruits and vegetables used are directly from the partners so that they can be found right from the planting process to the introduction.

Fish and other marine animals

Because Japanese cuisine is almost made up of fish, of course the quality of the fish used is very important. The fish used are directly delivered by the fishermen to the Foodies kitchen to ensure the freshness of the fish. Likewise with other marine animals, such as shrimp and crabs.
