Foodies is a Japanese restaurant that has been established since October 2016. Foodies also has many branches spread throughout Indonesia.


In the food production process, Foodies cooperates with local farmers to meet the supply of raw materials, so that the food ingredients used are always fresh and guaranteed quality.

Our


Foodies provides Japanese specialties, ranging from ramen to sushi with extraordinary flavors because they are cooked directly by Japanese chefs. In addition to cuisine, there are also Japanese snacks and drinks which will certainly make the atmosphere like in Japan.

In addition to providing Japanese specialties, Foodies also provides Academy or Japanese food cooking training. The teacher is a professional chef who was brought in from Japan directly to practice the techniques and maintain the taste of Japanese food.
